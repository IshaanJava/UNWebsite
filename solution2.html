What Can You Do?

Afghanistan needs help, this war-stricken nation and its people have endured so much during their lifetimes. Now, they need help to rebuild and improve their country. This help can be as simple as donating a dollar a month, to going overseas to deliver humanitarian aid. Every bit of aid is another stride in creating a better Afghanistan, and an overall better world. Please consider these charities for your donations. More Info

Information Sources & Charities

Wildlife Conservation Society

WCS is an all rounded organization that takes donations and helps wildlife in almost every continent. They have provided huge efforts in saving the snow leopards in Afghanistan, among other species. Learn More

UN World Food Programme

The UN World Food Programme is one of the world's largest humanitarian organizations. They provide relief to countries in need of food assistance, such as Afghanistan. Learn More

Women for Afghan Women

Women for Afghan Women is a grassroots organization that supports women and girls around Afghanistan. They have distributed thousands of aid packages throughout Afghanistan and refurbished educational facilities. Learn More
